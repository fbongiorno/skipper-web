Practical Information

The Caribbean is the world's premier winter yachting hub. Mid-December to April offers the best weather and reliable trade winds.

Varies by island, but the average luxury catamaran starts at $25,000, while motor yachts are $50,000 and up.

The British Virgin Islands are the global sailing capital. St. Barths and Antigua offer elite superyacht glamour.

Logistics vary widely between sovereign islands. USD is king, but the Euro rules in French territories like Guadeloupe and St. Martin.
