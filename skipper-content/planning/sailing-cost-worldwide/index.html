How to Get the Best Value

  • Travel shoulder season: May/June or September saves 30-50% on charter rates in the Mediterranean.
  • Larger group: Splitting a 44ft catamaran between 8 people cuts per-person costs by 33% vs 6 people.
  • Choose a monohull: Equivalent-length monohulls are typically 35-50% cheaper than catamarans.
  • Cook on board: Cooking 4 out of 7 dinners on board saves $400-$700 per group versus eating out every night.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest sailing holiday destination in the world?

For quality sailing in a stunning location, Turkey and Montenegro offer the best value globally. In the Caribbean, the Grenadines and Martinique/Guadeloupe offer the best value.

Is a sailing holiday cheaper than a hotel-based beach holiday?

Split between 6-8 people, a catamaran charter in Greece costs roughly $900-$1,300 per person for a week - comparable to a mid-range hotel, but with a different anchorage every night.
