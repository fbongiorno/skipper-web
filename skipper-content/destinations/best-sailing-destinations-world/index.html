Mediterranean

Greece sits at the top of almost every poll. Croatia is Europe's fastest-rising destination. Turkey's turquoise coast offers Aegean sailing at 30% lower cost.

Caribbean

The BVI are the world's most popular bareboat destination. The Grenadines offer the Caribbean's most spectacular scenery. The Windward Islands reward with far fewer crowds.

Pacific & Indian Ocean

The Whitsundays in Australia combine the Great Barrier Reef with reliable SE trade winds. French Polynesia — Tahiti, Bora Bora, the Tuamotus — is bucket-list sailing.

How to choose

Beginners: BVI or Ionian Islands. Intermediate: Croatia or Saronic Gulf. Advanced: Cyclades in summer.

Frequently asked questions

What is the most popular sailing destination?
The BVI consistently rank as the world's most popular bareboat destination.
Where is the best sailing in Europe?
Greece is widely considered Europe's finest.
When is the best time to sail the Caribbean?
Mid-November to late April, avoiding hurricane season.
