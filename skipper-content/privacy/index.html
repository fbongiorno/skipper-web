Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Skipper ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit skipper.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

This is a general template and does not constitute legal advice. You should have it reviewed by a qualified professional to ensure it meets the requirements applicable to your business and jurisdiction.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the email address you submit. When you use our trip planner or contact us, we may collect the details you choose to provide.

Information collected automatically: Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and analytics, including your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve the Site, respond to your enquiries, send you our newsletter and related content where you have subscribed, understand how visitors use the Site through aggregated analytics, and protect the security and integrity of the Site.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable core Site functionality and to gather analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand Site usage; Google may set cookies and process data in accordance with its own privacy policy. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly as a result.

Email and Marketing

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we will use your email address to send you content and updates. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link, and you may opt out at any time. We do not sell your email address to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Site relies on third-party services including our hosting provider, analytics, and image providers. These services may process limited technical data as necessary to deliver their functionality. We also link to external websites; we are not responsible for the privacy practices of sites we do not operate.

Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us through the Site.
